projects
Data-Driven Selection of Representative Motion Models for Simulation in Dynamic Cardiac PET develops trajectory analysis, image processing, quantitative evaluation, and computational simulation pipelines for realistic PET motion simulations.
PET/CT VR Volume Rendering and Visualization develops an immersive PC-VR workflow for dynamic PET/CT inspection, medical volume rendering, DICOM/NIfTI-style data handling, transfer-function tuning, and motion-analysis review.
MPI-SPECT Segmentation Pipeline: an end-to-end deployable 3D medical-imaging software pipeline using U-Net3D for SPECT volume segmentation, preprocessing, inference, DAE refinement, and morphological post-processing.
Privacy-aware federated ECG digitization is an end-to-end framework for ECG page processing without centralizing sensitive clinical images.
A fully automated ARUNet pipeline for ECG print-image digitization performs preprocessing, segmentation, waveform reconstruction, and transformer-based refinement.
CNNs for multi-frame stock-market prediction combined models trained on several time horizons for financial time-series forecasting.
