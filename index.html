About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am currently a research intern under the supervision of Dr. JafariTadi at the University of Turku, where I focus on advancing image and signal processing techniques to develop cutting-edge methods for ECG digitalization. Prior to this role, I worked as a project researcher at the University of Turku, specializing in signal processing and utilizing deep learning models to detect heart abnormalities.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science, where my research involved innovative approaches to stock price prediction by combining multiple time frames. My academic journey began with a BSc in Mathematics, laying the foundation for my expertise in computational and analytical methods.
